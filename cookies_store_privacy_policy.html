Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 9, 2025

Thank you for using the Cookies Store Chrome Extension. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information or browsing data. The extension operates entirely on the user's browser and does not transmit or save any private information.

2. Cookie Usage

The extension fetches publicly accessible cookie data from a remote server based on the domain you are currently visiting. These cookies are imported into your browser only upon your request, and are not stored or analyzed by us.

3. Remote Requests

To provide its core functionality, the extension sends a request to a remote API (e.g., https://example.com/cookie_store/api.php) to retrieve domain-specific cookie data. No personal or sensitive user data is included in these requests.

4. Permissions

The extension requires browser permissions to access tabs, cookies, and active websites to operate correctly. These permissions are used strictly for importing cookies and are never misused.

5. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at support@example.com.

By using the Cookies Store extension, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
